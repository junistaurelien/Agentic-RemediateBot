# Remediation Runbook Template (Agentic-RemediateBot)

**Date Context:** 2026-02-12 (Simulated)

## Objective
Provide a repeatable remediation workflow with approval gates, rollback steps, and validation evidence.

## Workflow
1. Ingest vulnerability backlog
2. Prioritize by risk/exposure
3. Draft remediation plan + change request
4. Human approval (CAB)
5. Execute remediation
6. Validate + capture evidence
7. Closeout reporting

## Evidence Requirements
- Patch/version proof
- Health checks
- Change record
- Stakeholder sign-off
